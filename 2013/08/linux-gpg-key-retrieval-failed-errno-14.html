[linux]修复 GPG key retrieval failed: [Errno 14] Could not open/read file:///etc/pki/rpm-gpg/RPM-GPG-KEY-rpmforge-dag

Date Fri 02 August 2013 Category linux Tags linux / rpmforge

两种方法：

  • 编辑 /etc/yum/etc/yum.repos.d/rpmforge.repo，更改 gpgcheck=0

  • rpm --import http://apt.sw.be/RPM-GPG-KEY.dag.txt
